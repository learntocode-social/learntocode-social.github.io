April Code Sprint, April 2nd - 26th

       
       

Get started programming this month of April. You'll learn the basics of Web development and practice thinking like a programmer along with hundreds of people participating in week long sprints.

There'll be regular office hours with mentors who are experts in their respective fields to answer your questions everyday at 6 P.M. And along with the excitement of learning something new, after successful completion you'll also receive the RSPCT Certificate of Accomplishment.

HTML & CSS Sprint, April 2nd - 5th

       

HTML & CSS are the fundamental building blocks of the Web. HTML defines the structure of your Website and CSS controls the look and feel. In this sprint you will go through Codecademy's HTML & CSS track and then Make a Website.

Javascript Sprint, April 10th - 15th

Javscript breathes life into your Website. With HTML & CSS your Website will be static, but with Javascript you can add interactivity to your site and make it pop. In this sprint you will go through Codecademy's Javascript track and then Make an Interactive Website.

HackerRank Sprint, April 18th - 21st

Designing elegant solutions requires you to think and approach problems from different perspectives. In this sprint you'll practice thinking like a programmer. You'll go through the challenges in HackerRank and get an Algorithms Badge.
