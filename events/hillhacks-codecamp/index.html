HillHacks CodeCamp, May 5th - June 7th

HillHacks is a gathering of Techies, Artists, Makers, and Entrepreneurs collaborating on various activities in the Himalayas (Dharmashala to be exact). This HillHacks we are conducting a CodeCamp from May 5th - June 7th. You'll learn to build Web and Mobile Apps and work with others in various projects. You are welcome to participate.

The FAQ on the HillHacks Website is very comprehensive. Please read it to get a general idea of what to expect when participating in HillHacks, and other relevant information such as travel, accomodation, costs etc.
